WELCOME!

My name is Victor Skillerstrand and I am an 18 year old programmer from Sweden. Programming has been a hobby of mine for over a year now and I can honestly say it has genuinly changed my view on games, websites and programs in generall. Each and every time I start a game or visit a page, I immediately want to know how that was created; what language it has been written in, how many individual programmers and/or designers worked to achieve it and so on. By doing this my interest in creating a indie-game or a website sparked. Even this website was made in HTML & CSS by me!

The purpose of this website is to show what I have done and what I am capable of doing. On the "Projects" page you can see some of my earlier creations (mostly games). The "Programming" page contains my knowledge of certain languages and what I am currently learning, as well as my progress in those particular languages.

If you or your team are interested in working with me in any way feel free to contact me on the bottom right side of this page. I generally respond quite fast but it might vary from day to day.

Hope to hear from you and/or your team soon, cheers!

- Victor Skillerstrand
